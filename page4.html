There are three types of Indias. The "Bullock cart India" that comprises of the poorest of the poor, striving with each passing day to earn approximately a dollar and to be able to save money for themselves and their family.

Next, there is the "Scooter India". Scooter India reflects the middle class society.

The third kind of India is the "Airplane India" that reflects the rich in the society.

It is with the booming of Information Technology in India, that there are more people willing to pursue higher studies in the United States or are coming to the United States for work. And eventually Indians end up immigrating to the United States once they have had an exposure to the culture. Most Indian born immigrants mostly comprise of the Airplane India.
